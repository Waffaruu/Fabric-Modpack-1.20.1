first click on fabric installer.
make sure to download right minecraft version inn it (1.20.1)
and click ''install''
should be over pretty quick.



open your Original ''.minecraft'' folder by writing ''%appdata%'' in your windows searchbar.
then open the ''.minecraft'' in the modpack folder and copy over all the folder to your original folder.

then start up minecraft :)


pro tip: in minecraft launcher u can allocate more ram to the game if u feel its a little bit unstable.
check the pictures in ''HowtoRam'' folder.
PS: the ram is in Gigabyte, i choose 15 cause i have 32, 4gb-6gb is recomended.
